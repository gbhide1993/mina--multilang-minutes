Cancellation & Refund Policy

1. Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your MinA subscription at any time. To cancel, simply send the command "Cancel Subscription" to the MinA WhatsApp bot or use the manage subscription link provided in your billing email.

Upon cancellation, your access to Premium features will continue until the end of your current billing cycle.

2. Refund Policy

Since MinA offers a Free Tier to try the service before purchasing:

  • Monthly Subscriptions: We generally do not offer refunds for partial months of service.
  • Technical Issues: If you are unable to use the service due to a technical error on our end that we cannot resolve within 48 hours, you are eligible for a full refund of the current month's fee.

3. Processing Refunds

Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days and credited back to the original payment method.
